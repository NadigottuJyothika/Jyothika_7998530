Community Bulletin

Welcome to the weekly community bulletin! It has been an exciting month for our neighborhood. With the recent completion of the downtown park renovations, families have been flocking to the new playground and picnic areas. We encourage everyone to keep the park clean and utilize the new recycling bins provided by the city council.

In other news, the local library is hosting a summer reading program for children of all ages. Volunteer sign-ups are now open for the upcoming charity run, and we are expecting record participation this year. Please ensure you register early if you plan to run or volunteer. Lastly, don't forget the town hall meeting next Wednesday where we will discuss the proposed cycling lanes along Main Street. Your voice matters, so please try to attend or submit your feedback online.

As a reminder, street sweeping will occur on the first and third Tuesdays of the month. Please remember to move your vehicles off the street on those days to avoid ticketing. If you have questions regarding the sweeping schedule, contact the community center.
